How to Use the Application

  • Choose Mode: Select ‘Encrypt’ or ‘Decrypt’.
  • Load Files: Choose the files you need to process.
  • Process: Hit ‘Process’. Once processing is complete, save buttons will appear.
  • Save Files: Click to save files to your device.
  • Secure Key: Store the encryption key in a secure location.
  • Reset: After encryption, press ‘Reset’ to clear sensitive data.
  • Safety Reminder: Never leave your computer unattended after encryption without hitting ‘Reset’.
  • Decryption: Use the key and the encrypted file together in ‘Decrypt’ mode to revert to the original file.
  • Key Caution: Using a different key will result in decryption failure.

Your Data Privacy

Your files are processed locally on your device. No data is uploaded to servers, ensuring your information remains confidential in the security of your computer.
